How To Use This Application

This application helps you understand and interact with basic cryptography concepts including the Caesar Cipher and AES encryption.

Caesar Cipher

To use the Caesar Cipher section:

  • Input your text in the text area, e.g., "HELLO WORLD".
  • Choose a shift number, e.g., 3.
  • Click 'Encrypt' to encrypt the text, which will result in "KHOOR ZRUOG".
  • To decrypt, enter the encrypted text and the same shift number, then click 'Decrypt'.

AES Encryption

In the AES section:

  • Input your plaintext, e.g., "Hello, AES!"
  • Enter a key (16, 24, or 32 bytes), e.g., "mysecretkey123456".
  • Click 'Encrypt' to encrypt your text. You will see the encrypted text in the output area.
  • To decrypt, enter the encrypted text and the same key, then click 'Decrypt' to see the original plaintext.
